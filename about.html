О НАС

Наша компания

Camso is the best of Camoplast and Solideal

By bringing together Camoplast and Solideal as Camso the Road Free Company, we are preserving the values and history that define us, while keeping an eye on the future. As well as sending out a strong message that we are one team dedicated to being a world-class brand manufacturer and service provider of performance products to meet all your off-the-road vehicle needs.

We are a one-stop shop

We serve the material handling, construction, agriculture and powersport industries by manufacturing and distributing pneumatic, airless and solid tires, tracks, driven and trailed conversion track systems and OEM undercarriages. We even operate our own foundry and go right to the source of rubber. We work from the tree to the tread.

WE OFFER LOCS
We bring the lowest operating cost solution to the off-the-road mobility market.

We believe that to truly serve our customers' off-the-road business, we need to free ourselves from the road. We need to be Road Free.
